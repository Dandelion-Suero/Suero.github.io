I used the program Adobe Photoshop to create my character, Skia. I started by looking at my old works and seeing what characters I've made but never expanded on. I really wanted to make a hero story but with a bit of a fun twist. So instead of a human seeking out greater power I made a shadow person who happened to be in the right place at the right time. Skia starts out with a tattered cloak and a rank 1 traveler status. Skia has a commission to find a dog that ran off into the woods, while in their search they find the legendary sword that talks!
